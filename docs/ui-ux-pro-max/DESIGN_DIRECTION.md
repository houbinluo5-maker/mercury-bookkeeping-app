# Design Direction

## North Star
Executive Finance OS: calm, premium, precise, CPA-friendly, founder-friendly, and operationally useful.

## Reference Qualities
- Ramp: crisp finance command center.
- Mercury: polished financial product restraint.
- Brex: operational card hierarchy.
- Stripe Dashboard: clean controls and dense data.
- Linear: refined spacing and focus states.

## Visual Principles
- Premium restraint: no loud gradients or decoration for decoration's sake.
- Command-center hierarchy: every page should answer what needs attention and what action comes next.
- Trust through structure: clear panels, explicit statuses, safe destructive separation.
- Data density with breathing room: tables remain useful, but filters and actions are better grouped.
- CPA-ready presentation: reports and exports should feel deliverable, not raw.

## Palette
- Background: warm off-white/light slate.
- Primary: deep navy finance blue.
- Accent: muted teal.
- Success: soft green.
- Warning: amber.
- Danger: muted red.
- Text: strong slate/navy.
- Borders: soft neutral gray.

## Interaction Tone
Buttons should feel intentional. Primary actions stand out, secondary actions recede, and destructive actions remain visually separated. Filter bars should feel like professional controls, not loose form fields.
